About Us

About Us

Pasumai bharathi trust is a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering environment sustainability and promoting social welfare in our communities. Established with the vision of creating a greener and more harmonious world, We are committed to addressing environmental challenges while improving the quality of life for underprivileged sections of society.

Our work is centered around a eco system approach, blending environment conservation with social upliftment. We believe that a sustainable future can only be achieved by taking care of both our planet and its people.

Our Mission

To create a greener and healthier environment for future generations and disease free, While empowering communities through education, healthcare and socio-economic development.

Our Vision

To become a leading force for environmental stewardship and social responsibility fostering a future where nature and humanity co exist in harmony.

What We Do

At pasumai bharathi trust, we are dedicated to supporting various stakeholders in our eco system.

Local Communities

We empower individuals and groups by providing education and resources to adopt sustainable practices that protect their environment.

Farmers and Agricultural workers

We promote eco-friendly farming techniques that enhance soil health and biodiversity while ensuring food security.

Youth and Students

Through educational awareness programs, we inspire the next generation to become environmental stewards and advocates for sustainability.

Conservationists and Environmental Organizations

We collaborate with like-minded organizations to drive impactful projects that restore habitats and protect endangered species.

Doctors

In addition to treating patients, doctors help raise awareness about preventive health care measures. They educate communities about the importance of hygiene, sanitation, nutrition and regular health check ups, to prevent disease like cancer and promote overall well being.

By working together with these groups, we aim to create a thriving eco system that benefits both people and the planet.
