Social Welfare

Diwali Celebration & Gift Distribution

29 October 2024 | Melattur Village, Thanjavur

Distributed gifts and essential items to families and children during Diwali celebrations, sharing joy and care with the local community.

Event Highlights

Community Support

Provided gift packs and daily essentials to underprivileged families across Melattur village.

Children's Joy

Engaged with children through celebration activities and ensured each child received a special gift.

Local Partnership

Worked with village volunteers and local organizers to reach families in need efficiently.
