Our Programs

Environmental Conservation

We actively engage in tree planting drives, promote organic farming, water conservation projects and waste management programs to reduce the ecological foot print.

Social Welfare

Pasumai bharathi trust is committed to enhancing the lives of the less fortunate by providing access to basic health care, education and livelihood opportunities.

Community Empowerment

We work closely with local communities to raise awareness about environmental issues and to encourage sustainable practices at the grass roots level.

Health & Wellness

Our health care camps and wellness initiatives are aimed at providing essential medical services to rural and underserved populations.

Finance and Accountability

At Pasumai Bharathi Trust, we believe that financial transparency and accountability are key to building trust with our donors, partners and the communities we serve. Our financial practices are designed to ensure that every contribution is utilized efficiently and effectively to achieve our mission of environmental sustainability and social welfare.
