Finance and Accountability

At pasumai bharathi trust, we believe that financial transparency and accountability are key to building trust with our donors, partners and the communities we serve. Our financial practice are designed to ensure that every contribution is utilized efficiently and effectively to achieve our mission of environmental sustainability and social welfare.

Utilization of Funds

We are committed to ensuring that funds are allocated effectively to maximize the impact of our programs. Our financial resources are directed toward:

Environmental Projects

A significant portion of our funds goes toward tree plantation drives, water conservation projects, organic farming initiatives and waste management programs.

Health & Wellness

We fund free medical camps, health education programs and health care services in underserved communities to improve public health.

Education & Awareness

Our awareness campaigns and educational workshops on environmental conservation and sustainable living are powered by donations and grants.

Community Empowerment

Financial support enables us to run livelihood programs, provide skill development training and support the economic growth of rural and marginalized communities.

Administrative Costs

A small percentage of funds is allocated to cover operational and administrative costs, ensuring that the trust functions smoothly and effectively.

Transparency and Reporting

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of transparency in our financial management. Pasumai bharathi trust ensures that all donations and funds are tracked and reported accurately. We provide:

Annual Financial Reports Donor Reports Audited Accounts

Impact of Contribution

Every contribution no matter in size has a direct impact on the lives of those we serve through responsible financial management. we ensure that donations are stretched to achieve maximum benefit for our environment and social welfare initiatives. For example:

₹500

plant 10 trees and contribute to our reforestation efforts

₹1000

provide medical treatment to a family during one of our health camps

₹5000

sponsor the education of a child for a year
